Leading Others: the Basics

Become effective team leaders through excellent mentoring, managing, developing, communicating, and creating cultures of growth and support.

Step 1

Understand the elements of an effective communications/coaching approach that emphasizes strong 2-way communication focused on the development of staff.

Step 2

Have strength-based career conversations with your direct reports that focus on growth.

Step 3

Gather different perspectives from your team prior to making decisions.

Step 4

Construct engaging narratives that will support your vision and inspire your team!

Step 5

Engage in meaningful and regular (quarterly) MyPerformance conversations with all team members.

Step 6

Build your team's capacity for collaboration and succession management.

Step 7

Support team members to take temporary assignments and get involved in projects.

Step 8

Create a culture of trust, innovation and inclusion, where perspectives are accepted from everyone and doing things differently is encouraged.

Step 9

Connect your staff when they're at a distance - effectively managing a virtual team or teams with remote workers.
