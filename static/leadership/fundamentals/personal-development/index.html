Topic: Leadership Fundamentals

Personal Development

Engage in personal development activities that grow your skills as a leader.

Step 1

Recognize your own health and wellbeing as an imperative, not just a "nice to have." Wellness is required of leaders.

Step 2

Analyze your strengths and supports. In this step, you'll identify the strengths and supports that promote your resiliency and ability to navigate challenge and adversity, and identify and articulate the supports you still need to be fully resilient.

Step 3

Develop humility through authentic self-awareness.

Step 4

Understand personal identity and examine unconscious bias for areas of growth.

Step 5

Build capacity to effectively receive and use feedback.

Step 6

Create opportunities outside of your role for practical application of leadership skills, and design your own evaluation to inform future growth.

Step 7

Develop a plan to build a mentorship relationship.

Step 8

Identify personal strengths and areas for development as a leader.

Step 9

Develop the confidence and presence required of leadership according to your own style, through practice and acting on feedback from others.

Step 10

Develop an awareness of leadership styles and articulate your personal philosophy of leadership.
