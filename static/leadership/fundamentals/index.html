Leadership Fundamentals

These pathways focus on building a solid personal foundation—whether your interest is in developing your skills where you are right now or to develop for future roles.

Leading Others: the Basics

Looking for support with team leader responsibilities? Curious about what’s required to be successful in a supervisor or management role? Follow this pathway for support on effectively leading teams of all kinds; managing change, conflict, and risk; building relationships and managing team dynamics; and creating a culture of trust and innovation.

Personal Development

Ready to engage in personal development that grows your skills as a leader? Whether your goals are to advance to another position or to lead from where you are, this pathway will take you on a journey to improve your ability to lead from where you are and to reach your potential in the future.

Role Advancement

Start feeling in control of your BC Public Service career path. Whether you’re ready to move into a new role now or in a few years, this pathway can help you get clear on your next steps, connect with mentorship you need, and set your own goals.

Functions of Government

How can building an understanding of legislation and policy help you to be an effective leader? How can you go beyond Gov 101 to deepen your knowledge of the functions of government and find out how major government initiatives impact your role and those you lead or may lead in the future? Follow this pathway to understand what you need to know now.
